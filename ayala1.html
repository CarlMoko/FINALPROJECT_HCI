Ayala Malls Harbor Point

Ayala Mall Harbor Point SBMA is a popular shopping destination located in Subic Bay Freeport Zone in Zambales, Philippines. About

Ayala Mall Harbor Point is a premier shopping and lifestyle destination located inside the Subic Bay Freeport Zone
in the Philippines. It offers a wide variety of retail, dining, and entertainment options, making it a popular choice for locals
and tourists alike. With its convenient location near the Subic Bay International Airport and major highways, Ayala Mall Harbor
Point is a vibrant hub for shopping, dining, and leisure activities in the Subic Bay area.
