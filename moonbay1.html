About

Moonbay Marina is a leisure and entertainment complex located in the Subic Bay Freeport Zone in the Philippines.
The marina offers a range of amenities and activities for visitors, including boat rentals, water sports, dining options, and
event spaces for various occasions. With its scenic views of the bay and bustling atmosphere, Moonbay Marina is a popular
destination for locals and tourists alike. Whether you're looking to relax by the water or enjoy some exciting activities,
Moonbay Marina has something for everyone.
