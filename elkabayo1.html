ElKabayo Waterfalls

El Kabayo Waterfalls is a beautiful natural attraction located in the Subic Bay Metropolitan Authority (SBMA) in the Philippines. The waterfall is named after the horses that used to be kept in the area, and it is a popular spot for locals and tourists alike to cool off and enjoy the beauty of nature. With its crystal-clear waters and lush green surroundings, El Kabayo Waterfalls is a tranquil and refreshing destination for those seeking a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

About

El Kabayo Waterfalls is a popular tourist destination located in Subic Bay Freeport Zone in the Philippines.
The waterfall is named after the El Kabayo Stables, which were established in Subic Bay Freeport Zone in the 1990s. The stables
were known for offering horseback riding activities to tourists and locals alike.
In recent years, El Kabayo Waterfalls has become a must-visit attraction in Subic Bay due to its stunning natural beauty and tranquil
surroundings. Visitors can enjoy swimming in the clear waters of the falls, as well as hiking through the lush rainforest to reach the falls.
The waterfall has also become a popular spot for picnics and relaxation, as visitors can unwind and enjoy the peaceful ambiance of the area.
El Kabayo Waterfalls is a testament to the natural beauty and biodiversity of the Philippines, and continues to attract visitors from all over the world.
